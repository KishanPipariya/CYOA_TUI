# Shadows of the Forgotten Prison (10)

You awaken in a dimly lit, cold dungeon cell. The air is thick with the scent of damp stone and despair. Chains hang from the ceiling, and the walls are slick with moisture. You can feel your heart pounding against your ribcage, echoing the fear that grips you. The only light comes from a flickering torch mounted outside your cell, casting long, ominous shadows. You try to remember how you got here, but only fragmented memories of a dark night and a betrayal linger.

> **You chose:** Search the cell for any items that might help you escape or survive a little longer.

---

You begin to search the cell thoroughly, your hands feeling the cold stone walls and the floor. In a corner, you find a small, rusted dagger. It's not much, but it could be useful. There's also a thin strip of metal that might be bendable. You also notice a small pouch hidden under a pile of straw, but it's empty. As you continue your search, you realize that there's not much else of use in this bare cell.
