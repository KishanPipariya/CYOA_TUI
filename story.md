# Chains of Despair

You awaken in a dimly lit, cold stone cell. The air is thick with the stench of despair and the faint sounds of distant screams echo through the stone walls. You are chained to the wall, and the only other occupant is a haggard figure in the corner, their eyes never leaving you. The cell is sparse, with only a rusted shackle pin lying on the floor near your feet.

> **You chose:** Attempt to use the rusted shackle pin to pick the lock.

---

As you fumble with the rusted shackle pin, the cold metal feels almost alien in your hands. The lock is old and rusted, but the pin is just long enough to fit into the keyhole. With each attempt, the sound of the pin scraping against the lock echoes through the cell, heightening the tension. After several failed attempts, you finally hear the satisfying click as the lock gives way.
