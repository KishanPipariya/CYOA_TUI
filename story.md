# Prisoner of the Underworld (4)

You awaken in a cold, damp dungeon cell, the walls slick with moisture and the air thick with the stench of decay. Your hands are bound tightly behind your back, and your feet are chained to the floor. The only source of light is a dim torch flickering in the corner of the cell. You feel disoriented and confused, struggling to remember how you ended up here. Your only possessions are a small, rusty key hanging from a string around your neck and the tattered remnants of the cloak you're wearing. You must decide your next course of action to escape this place.

> **You chose:** Carefully inspect the cell, looking for any weak points or hidden mechanisms that could be used to escape. Gain 'Map Fragment' item if successful, indicating a secret passage exists nearby.

---

You take a few moments to carefully examine the cell. The walls are made of rough-hewn stone, and the floor is cold and hard. As you inspect the walls, you notice a small, barely visible crack near the bottom. You kneel down and use your hands to feel along the crack, finding a loose stone that gives way when you apply pressure. Inside, you find a fragment of a map, its edges tattered and worn, but the image of a hidden passage is clear.

> **You chose:** Investigate the loose stone further to see if it leads to a passage or secret room. Gain 'Loose Stone' item if successful, allowing access to a hidden passage.

---

You carefully remove the loose stone from the wall, revealing a dark, narrow passage behind it. The air is musty, and you can barely make out the faint glow of a distant torch. The map fragment you found earlier confirms that this passage leads somewhere beyond the confines of your cell. You carefully place the loose stone in your inventory to use later.
