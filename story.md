# Shadows of the Forgotten Prison (3)

You awaken in a dimly lit, cold dungeon cell. The air is thick with the scent of damp stone and despair. Chains hang from the ceiling, and the walls are slick with moisture. You can feel your heart pounding against your ribcage, echoing the fear that grips you. The only light comes from a flickering torch mounted outside your cell, casting long, ominous shadows. You try to remember how you got here, but only fragmented memories of a dark night and a betrayal linger.

> **You chose:** Search the cell for any items that might help you escape or survive a little longer.

---

As you search the cell, your fingers brush against the rough stone walls, feeling for any signs of weakness or hidden compartments. After a thorough examination, you find a small, rusted key wedged between two stones near the floor. It's not much, but it could be your ticket out of here if it fits the cell door.

> **You chose:** Continue searching the cell to see if there are any other useful items you might have missed before.

---

As you continue your search, you examine every nook and cranny of the cell. You find a small, torn piece of cloth and a crumpled, old piece of parchment with some faded writing on it. The cloth could be useful for bandaging wounds, while the parchment might contain clues or a map. However, you do not find any other keys or items that could aid your escape.
